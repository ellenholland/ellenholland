Digital Women

Digital Women is a club dedicated to women in computing majors. Our goal is to provide a comfortable environment for women to network with companies and other women in their majors. Our meetings consist of company visits, social activities, or .

As secretary I am in charge of taking meeting notes and sending (exciting) emails. As Vice President, I help the President with decision making and reminding/notifying executives and members of important events or tasks that need to be done. I am also the founder of Digital Women's Tumblr blog. I am also the Webmaster, in which I maintain and update our club website.
