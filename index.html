Digital Women

Digital Women is a club dedicated to women in computing majors. This includes but of course is not limited to majors such as Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. Our goal is to provide a comfortable environment for women to network with companies and other women in their majors. Each meeting consists of either a social activity, a craft activity (such as painting or soldering a Christmas tree), or a company visit.

As secretary I am in charge of taking meeting notes and sending (exciting) emails. As Vice President, I help the President with decision making and reminding/notifying executives and members of important events or tasks that need to be done. I am also the founder of Digital Women's Tumblr. A simple blog where people follow eachother, like, blog, or re-blog posts. This website shows some insight on my famous club emails.
