A little about me

I grew up and went to school in a small town in Iowa called Mount Vernon. In high school I took part in Band and Robotics club. It was in these two disciplines that I discovered a love of creating amazing and beautiful things while learning to challenge their difficulties.

I am now a student at Iowa State University studying Computer Science. I am the Vice President, Secretary and webmaster of Digital Women, a club for women in computing majors. Since coming to University I have learned more from my peers and experiences than I ever thought to learn in my classes. It truly is an amazing opportunity.
