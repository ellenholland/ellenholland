Digital Women

I am the Vice President and Secretary of Digital Women. Digital Women is a club dedicated to women in computing majors, which include but of course are not limited to majors such as Computer Science, Software Engineering,Computer Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. Our goal is to provide a comfortable environment for women to network with companies and other women in their majors. Each meeting consists of either a social activity, a craft activity (such as painting or soldering a "Love Bug")
